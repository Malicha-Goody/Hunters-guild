About Us

At Hunters Guild, we believe thriving communities are built by extraordinary people who bring diverse skill sets to drive collective growth.

Why We Exist

In today’s digital world, community is everything. Whether it's a vibrant Discord server, a buzzing Telegram group, or a dedicated subreddit, every successful community needs a dedicated manager to guide, support, and amplify its voice.

We created this platform to make hiring skilled community managers easy, fast, and trustworthy.

🧑‍💻 Our Responsibility as Your Web3 Community Manager / Moderator

  • 🧩 Community Engagement: Foster active engagement on Discord, Telegram, Twitter, etc. Start conversations, answer questions, and build a positive atmosphere.
  • 🛡️ Moderation: Enforce rules, handle conflicts, and remove spam, scams, or toxic behavior.
  • 📣 Communication & Announcements: Share official updates, events, and explain product features in simple terms.
  • 🎧 Feedback Collection: Monitor community sentiment and forward feedback, bugs, and feature requests to the team.
  • 🤝 Onboarding & Support: Welcome new members, provide guides, FAQs, and assist with platform usage.
  • 🗓️ Event Planning: Organize AMAs, giveaways, contests, and collaborative events.
  • 📊 Reporting: Deliver regular reports on engagement, growth, and user feedback.
  • 🚨 Crisis Management: Respond to FUD, bugs, or incidents transparently and calmly.
  • 🌍 Localization & Diversity: Support global communities by coordinating with local moderators and ensuring inclusivity.
  • Foster Collaboration: Ensuring that strong collaborations are established within communities to drive collective success.

Our Vision

We envision a future where community builders are truly valued, supported, and celebrated. Whether you’re scaling a DAO, launching a startup, or growing a fanbase, we’re here to help you build, manage, and nurture thriving communities.
